Fak'ugesi African Digital & Innovation Festival

Who
We Are

Fak'ugesi — meaning "electricity" in Zulu — is Africa's premier digital arts and innovation festival. Founded in 2014 and hosted at the Tshimologong Digital Innovation Precinct in Braamfontein, Johannesburg, Fak'ugesi has grown into a landmark platform that celebrates the intersection of technology, creativity, and African culture.

Each year, the festival brings together Africa's most inventive digital artists, technologists, designers, entrepreneurs, and thinkers for a week-long programme of exhibitions, performances, workshops, panels, and awards. Our reach spans the continent and beyond — connecting African creativity with global audiences and industry leaders.

Fak'ugesi is a space for bold ideas, authentic storytelling, and transformative encounters. We believe that African digital culture is not merely catching up with the world — it is leading it. The festival exists to prove exactly that.
