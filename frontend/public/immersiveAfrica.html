Immersive Experiences

Immersive Africa is Fak'ugesi's flagship programme celebrating African digital art in its most immersive form — from 360° dome screenings at the Wits Anglo American Digital Dome to interactive installations that blur the boundary between art and experience.

Kancícà Kwasukasukela

A breathtaking dome film that weaves together ancestral mythology and digital innovation. Premiering in South Africa exclusively at the Wits Anglo American Digital Dome as part of Immersive Africa. The 42-minute immersive experience draws on oral traditions from across the continent.

Ongoing Dome Screenings

Kwasukasukela — meaning "once upon a time" — presents a curated anthology of short films and works that centre African stories in immersive formats. Screenings run daily at the Wits Anglo American Digital Dome from 07–21 April 2026.
