FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Are tickets refundable?
Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the festival. Within 30 days, tickets can be transferred to another person.
How do I prove student status?
A valid student card or letter from your institution is required at registration. Student tickets are only available at the door or via email application.
Is the venue accessible?
Yes. The Newtown Cultural Precinct is fully wheelchair accessible. Please contact us in advance if you require specific accommodations.
Can I attend online?
Selected keynotes and panels will be livestreamed. A digital access pass will be available closer to the festival. Sign up for our newsletter for updates.
What's included in the expo?
The expo features 80+ exhibitors across digital art, technology, gaming, fintech, health tech and more. It's included in all passes.
Is there group booking?
Groups of 10+ receive a 15% discount. Contact us at groups@fakugesi.co.za for group booking arrangements.
