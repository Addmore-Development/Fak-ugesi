FAQ

Frequently
Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about submitting to the Fak'ugesi Awards 2026. More questions? Email awards@fakugesi.co.za

Who can enter the Fak'ugesi Awards? +
The awards are open to African digital artists, designers and technologists based anywhere in the world. Entries must be primarily digital in nature and completed after January 2024.
When is the submission deadline? +
The submission deadline is 31 July 2026. Shortlist announced 15 August. Winners revealed at the Awards Ceremony on 12 October 2026.
Can I submit to multiple categories? +
Yes, multiple category submissions are welcome. Each category requires a separate submission form and entry materials.
What are the prizes? +
Cash prizes range from R20,000 to R40,000 per category. Winners also receive international residencies, commissions, fellowships and global press coverage.
Do I need to attend the festival? +
Shortlisted artists are strongly encouraged to attend the festival and Awards Ceremony in Johannesburg (7–12 October 2026). Travel bursaries may be available for international finalists.
Is there an entry fee? +
No. Submissions are completely free. There is no cost to enter the Fak'ugesi Awards in any category.
