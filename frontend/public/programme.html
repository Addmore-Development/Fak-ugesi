Fak'ugesi 2026 is structured around six flagship signature programmes, each targeting a distinct creative community. From award-winning digital art to gaming arcades, immersive dome experiences, industry networking, shark-tank pitches, and high-energy animation hackathons — every programme is a world unto itself.

Featured Events

01
Immersive Africa · Dome Premiere
Kancícà — South African Digital Dome Premiere

An immersive full-dome experience by Dream Feel Factory in co-production with Maison des Mondes Africains. Rooted in Central African cosmology and visual poetry, Kancícà takes audiences on a journey through ancestral memory and speculative future.

Dates: 07 – 21 Apr 2026 · Venue: Wits Digital Dome, Braamfontein
02
Fak'ugesi Awards · Ceremony
Awards Ceremony & Exhibition

The culmination of the festival — a celebration of Africa's best digital creative work across six categories. Works shortlisted by the 2026 international jury are exhibited throughout the festival week before winners are announced at the closing ceremony.

Ceremony: 12 Oct 2026 · Venue: Tshimologong, Braamfontein
03
JAMZ · Animation Challenge
Tournament of Power — Animation Final

The single-elimination animation tournament reaches its climax on Day 5. Teams that have survived three rounds of live judging face off in a final showdown. Audience votes combine with jury scores. R20,000 prize pool for the winning team.

Final Day: 11 Oct 2026 · Venue: Main Stage, Tshimologong
04
Pitchathon · Main Event
Pitchathon Grand Finale

Six shortlisted African digital startups face a panel of six industry jurors in a high-stakes, live pitch event. The winner takes home a R20,000 cash prize and a WIC Post-Graduate Diploma placement. The audience selects the People's Choice award.

Date: 10 Oct 2026 · Venue: Wits Innovation Centre
05
Fak'ugesiPRO · Panel
A Seat at the Table of Change

Returning from the 2025 highlights: this landmark panel brings together Thobile Chittenden, Samukelisiwe Dube, Masentle Nzimande, Tadeo Lopez-Sendon, Meghna Singh, and Daniel Getachew to discuss power, representation, and funding in Africa's creative tech economy.

Date: 08 Oct 2026 · Venue: Tshimologong Auditorium
06
Dala Khona · Arcade
Joburg Games Fest Pop-up

The beloved Joburg Games Fest returns as a curated activation within Dala Khona. Playable demos from 20+ African-made indie games, developer Q&As, retro arcade stations, and live speedrun competitions. Free with full festival pass.

Dates: 07 – 12 Oct 2026 · Venue: Tshimologong Courtyard
