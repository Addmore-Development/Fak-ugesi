FAQ

Are tickets refundable?+
Full refunds available up to 30 days before the festival. Within 30 days, tickets can be transferred to another person.
How do I prove student status?+
A valid student card or letter from your institution is required at registration. Student tickets are available at the door or via email application.
Is the venue accessible?+
Yes. The Newtown Cultural Precinct is fully wheelchair accessible. Please contact us in advance if you require specific accommodations.
Can I attend online?+
Selected keynotes and panels will be livestreamed. A digital access pass will be available closer to the festival.
Is there group booking?+
Groups of 10+ receive a 15% discount. Contact groups@fakugesi.co.za for group booking arrangements.
