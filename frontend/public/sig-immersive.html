Our Immersive Africa Partners

Immersive Africa is supported by the Spaces of Culture programme under Africa-Europe Partnerships for Culture, managed by EUNIC – EU National Institutes for Culture. This programme aims to contribute to improve Euro-African cultural cooperation, exchange, co-creation and dissemination around the two continents with the view to promoting intercultural dialogue and mutual understanding. By attending this event, you acknowledge and consent to photography and video recordings that may include your image. These materials may be used by Fak'ugesi African Digital Innovation Festival for marketing, promotional, archival, and communication purposes across our print and digital platforms, including social media.
