Requirements

Submit your application
Work must be created by an African national or diaspora participant with demonstrable ties to the continent. Both individual creators and collaborative teams are eligible to apply.
Entries must use digital tools as the primary medium — software, hardware, interactive, or immersive technology. Works combining analogue and digital methodologies are considered on a case-by-case basis.
Works older than two years from the submission date are ineligible. Works-in-progress that are substantially complete may be submitted with a note indicating expected completion date.
Portfolio, project description, high-resolution images, and an optional video link are all required. Incomplete submissions will not be considered for jury review.
